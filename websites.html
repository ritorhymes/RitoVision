William Entriken:
Digital Legacy Platform

www.WilliamEntriken.net

Rito worked with William Entriken, a polymathic businessman and technology pioneer who is one of the founding figures of the Non-fungible Token (NFT) eco-system, to create a website that would serve as a cornerstone for his legacy in digital assets. Rito's role was to not only design a digital experience but to help shape how the world perceives his contributions to the NFT space and the depth of impact that its had in culture and across multi-billion dollar industries.

Challenge
Solution

The challenge was to craft a narrative around William’s groundbreaking work in a way that was accessible to a wide audience, while still retaining the depth of his wide ranging technical contributions including as a Civic Hacker and cybersecurity specialist. My approach was to develop a digital framework that highlighted key milestones, connected his work to the broader evolution of NFTs, and positioned him as a thought leader in the space.

To meet this challenge, Rito conceived and developed comprehensive and multi-layered website that seamlessly blended storytelling with technical insights written in simple English making William Entriken's pioneering contributions both engaging and comprehensible to audiences of varying familiarity with NFTs and blockchain technology. An important secondary objective was also met aligning the content with the appropriate schemas for Search Engine Optimization (SEO) without structuring the content in a way that would feel inauthentic and deliberate.

Public Relations & Talent Agency Services

In a highly competitive industry, the challenge was to create a unified and impactful presence that clearly communicates their range of services while maintaining focus. Compromises

aConsultingAgency required a digital platform capable of encapsulating the multifaceted nature of their services—ranging from PR and talent management to branding and marketing—while representing a diverse roster that includes talents like George Clinton, YUME, and Ava Kolker. The primary challenge was to create a cohesive and impactful online presence that could clearly communicate the agency's wide array of services without overwhelming the user. In an industry marked by intense competition and rapid shifts, it was essential to design a website that not only stood out but also provided clarity amidst the complexity of their offerings. Balancing the need for a unified brand message with the individuality of each talent was critical to ensure both the agency and their clients were effectively represented.

The entertainment and influencer industry is increasingly leaning towards digital platforms that offer personalized and immersive experiences. There's a growing trend of agencies showcasing their portfolios through interactive media, leveraging high-quality visuals, and utilizing storytelling techniques to engage visitors. Additionally, with the rise of mobile browsing and social media integration, it's imperative for digital platforms to be responsive and seamlessly connected to various online channels. The trend towards simplicity and minimalism in design also plays a crucial role in highlighting content without distractions, ensuring that the focus remains on the talents and services offered.

To achieve a streamlined and user-friendly website, certain compromises were necessary. While it was tempting to include extensive details about every service and talent, we recognized that too much information could overwhelm visitors. Therefore, we opted for concise content with the option for users to delve deeper if they chose to. Additionally, balancing the individual branding of high-profile talents with the agency's overarching brand identity required careful consideration. We had to ensure that the website maintained a cohesive look and feel while allowing each talent's unique personality to shine through. This meant compromising on some customizations for individual talents in favor of a unified design language that benefited the overall user experience.
